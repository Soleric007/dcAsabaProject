Levels Of Restoration

There are three levels of restoration: 1.Spiritual restoration. 2.Mental restoration 3.Physical Restoration

1. SPIRITUAL RESTORATION. The death of Jesus restored all things to man. Rom 8:19-21. When you understand who you are circumstances of life start aligning with you. You are a new creature in Christ. You need to know you have been restored spiritually. The whole thing you are crying for is waiting for you. So we need to understand that God has restored us back to our original state and Glory. As believer's we have a personality, the personality of the Holy Ghost residing in us. The Holy Ghost cannot do anything until you stir up the spirit, through praying in tongues, studying the bible.

2. MENTAL RESTORTION. You can be restored spiritually and you are still functioning from your default state. The default state that does not have the Holy Spirit to help us. It is the work that determines the profit. God has restored you but do you stay on the word. The only way you can understand who you are is when you read the manual, the book to understand how we function and live our lives in this kingdom. The things we should bind and loose. Most times we keep on asking God for what he has already given us. Philp 4:8; Even though our outward man perishes our inward man is continually renewed. You can be asking God for restoration but your heart is not aligned with it.

3. PHYSICAL RESTORATION. The one everybody is crying for. You are declaring things you are asking God to restore but what is in your heart does not align. Some of us are collecting half and quarter restoration. Samuel 38:5-6, 11.

The first thing about restoration is revelation. You need to know what is God saying about this issue. How do you recover something when you don't know where it is. The first thing you need to know is what is God saying over this issue. Anytime you are calling for restoration you need to drop some things and people, it comes down to people and mindset. At times what you are looking for in restoration might not be what we find in our season of restoration. IN your season of restoration God brings relationship. It might be a Church where you resonate with. Be careful how you treat other people's children. Every time a relationship comes our way, we need to ask who do belong to and where are you coming from. This question is not for you to ask but for you to require of the holy spirit to discern this relationship. In your quest to restore. Whenever you are believing God for something be careful for different people come your way. At times we neglect the personality of the Holy Spirit that gives us direction. When we meet our destiny helper something clicks. Proverbs 6:30-31. When a thief goes to steal and he is caught the thief restores sevenfold. The same way when you are demanding for restoration you can demand for every other thing. What you don't claim is what you don't get. Overtaking is allowed. People are praying for restoration, and when God restores, they forget. Be careful when God restores you wave it out. This is the mystery. A lot of us get carried away. When you start thinking what God gave you is for you. it is a problem. What God gives you is for others, for the kingdom not just for you. That is why After dividing the spoils king David shared the spoils amongst all men. If you have a pastor honor your pastor, if you have a mentor, honor your mentor. That is how you keep these doors open for life. EBA, Emotional Bank Account. The higher people go the higher they get more emotional, the things they thought others can do you find nobody. That is why you can pray but until you understand the wisdom, the strategy for warfare. Men are channels don't play with men. Don't enter a door and close it because of greed. Don't pass through a door and close. Be careful how you treat people how you handle another man's business in your journey of your destiny. Restoration is because life goes in a circle. Ask God to open your eyes and after that you pray for wisdom. When you get to a level you have never attained before is to ask God for wisdom, on what to do and how to overcome that situation. One of the keys to restoration is WISDOM!
